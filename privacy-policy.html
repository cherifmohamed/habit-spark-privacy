Privacy Policy – HabitSpark

Last updated: 12/07/2025

Thank you for using HabitSpark. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

HabitSpark does not collect any personally identifiable information. All habits and data entered in the app are stored locally on your device only.

2. Advertising (AdMob)

The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect anonymous data to personalize ads based on user preferences.

You can review Google’s privacy policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Notifications

HabitSpark may send local notifications (e.g., habit reminders). No data is sent to any external server for this functionality.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties, except for data automatically managed by the integrated ad services (AdMob).

5. Security

All your data remains on your device. We recommend protecting your phone with a password or fingerprint to ensure your information stays safe.

6. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated. Any changes will be displayed in this section.

7. Contact

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at: contentflow.ai.team@gmail.com
